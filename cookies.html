Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your experience and understand how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To analyze website traffic and usage
  • To enhance security and prevent fraud
  • To provide a better, more personalized experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices and settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (like analytics) that set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Cookies Policy from time to time. Please review this page regularly for any changes.
